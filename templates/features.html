Guide

Input Parameters

Pregnancies

Number of times pregnant.

Normal:0-10
Higher counts increase risk

Glucose

Plasma glucose after 2h OGTT (mg/dL).

Normal:70-100
>126 = diabetes

Blood Pressure

Diastolic BP in mm Hg.

Normal:60-80
>90 = hypertension

Skin Thickness

Triceps skin fold thickness (mm).

Normal:10-50
Higher = obesity risk

Insulin

2-Hour serum insulin (mu U/mL).

Normal:16-166
Abnormal = resistance

BMI

Body Mass Index (kg/m2).

Normal:18.5-24.9
>30 = obese

Diabetes Pedigree

Genetic influence score.

Normal:0.0-2.5
>1.0 = genetic risk

Age

Age in years.

Any age
Risk rises after 45

Clinical Reference Values

Glucose

  • Normal: < 100 mg/dL
  • Prediabetes: 100-125
  • Diabetes: >= 126

Blood Pressure

  • Normal: < 120/80
  • Elevated: 120-129/80
  • High: >= 130/80

BMI

  • Normal: 18.5-24.9
  • Overweight: 25.0-29.9
  • Obese: >= 30.0

Age Risk

  • Low: < 35 years
  • Moderate: 35-50
  • High: > 50
